Beef Teriyaki

Ingredients

In this recipe we are using the following amount of ingredients per 100g. of lean beef

    5g. of sugar
    55g. of Kikoman soy sauce
    150g. of onion
    10g. of garlic powder
    10g. of olive oil
  • First prepare a pan and use a low amount of heat
  • Next, apply the 10g. of olive oil on the pan after 30 seconds of heating
  • Just cook the beef and mix everything bro
